How this application connects to a QuickBooks Online company, and who can connect it.

01What connecting does

Connecting is done through Intuit's secure OAuth sign-in. You choose the specific QuickBooks company to authorize, and the application can then access only that company, only for the scope you granted, and only while the authorization stays active. Your QuickBooks password is never seen by this application.

02Who can connect

This is a private application operated by Jonathan Hooker for The Innovation Barn and related accounting. It is not offered to the public and is not listed in the QuickBooks App Store. The operator authorizes the connection during setup; there is no public sign-up.

03Reconnecting

If access expires or is revoked, the operator re-authorizes through the same Intuit OAuth sign-in to restore the connection. To request help connecting or reconnecting, contact the operator below.
